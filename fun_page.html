The Fun Page: Freetime for students

TRAVEL THE CITY. If you want to experience the city quick without public transport we have citybikes and electric scooters which is amazing for getting around in places. you could travel to the end of aker brygge, take a ride along akershus festning or travel to Sørenga. seems interesting? see oslobysykkel.no for more info...

GOING TO BARS.Many students are new in Oslo, but dont be scared, we have plenty of good bars to go to when you finally have some freetime! there are alot of great bars with dart, shuffleboard, pool and much more. If you dont drink, then theese places can also be great for you. some we'd like to mention are: "Buddha bar", "Raadhuset" and "Blå"

CAMPUS ACTIVITIES.At OsloMet we have a wide range of courses at our makerspace. This allows you to learn skills like woodcrafting, sewing, 3D printing and more. Theese courses are free to attend and you can join as many as you like to gain as much knowledge as possible. you can even use the equipment as you like and make thing there.

STUDENT ASOCIATION.As a student you can join any student asociation you would like. There are many to choose from and can be everything from a new activity you have never tried to a activity you have done for years. The skill level among the students are huge and isnt what´s important. Everyone is there to make friends and have fun!

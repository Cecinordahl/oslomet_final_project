Fun Page

Fun page: Make one page that is in a different layout and have a different styling than the others. Put in some text and images. We accept place-holder text, but don’t use copyrighted images. Or you can present an activity or a place in Oslo. This page is an opportunity to be creative and shine. You may embed video or sound, but again: beware of copyright, please! Don’t forget to consider the size of the media files (videos or pictures).
